\# Phishing Email Analysis Report



\## Objective

Analyze a suspicious email and identify phishing indicators.



\---



\## Email Details



\*\*Sender\*\*

support@paypaI.com



\*\*Subject\*\*

Your Account Has Been Limited



\---



\## Phishing Indicators Found



\### 1. Email Spoofing

The sender's email looks like PayPal but uses "paypaI.com" instead of "paypal.com".



\### 2. Urgent Language

The email threatens account suspension within 24 hours.



\### 3. Suspicious Link

The URL



http://paypal-account-security-login.xyz



does not belong to the official PayPal domain.



\### 4. Generic Greeting

Uses "Dear Customer" instead of the user's real name.



\### 5. Social Engineering

Creates fear to force the user into clicking the malicious link.



\### 6. Grammar

Minor grammatical issues are present.



\---



\## Conclusion



This email is a phishing email because it uses spoofing, urgency, fake links, and social engineering to steal user credentials.
